About US
Principles of Jain dharma and Jain institutes (sangh) are well known for its progressive nature lead by visionaries (shravak and sadhu) who have ensured continuity (shashwat) of the dharma and its values by taking entire community together towards shifting nature of the modern realities at every critical juncture.
Donating towards Jain causes in a traditional manner has reached its limits and has limited appeal to the new generation of donors that are tech savvy and opt for convenience in every aspect of their life. The maturity of digital and mobile payment technologies in India offers perfect opportunity to become first mainstream religion to transform itself towards this new reality.
Jainity is founded based on the importance of daan (donation) in Jain dharma towards accumulating punya laabh (good karma) and shredding off paap (bad karma). With Jainity donors can now earn good karma instantly at their finger tips sitting anywhere in the world.
Jainity is yet another example of Jain community’s progressive nature by adapting to the modern realities for the eternity of Jain principles.
Jainity aims to become the most trustworthy giving platform for donors. Our guiding principles are based in teachings of Jain Dharma and its values. We are passionate about increasing good karma for all our stakeholders to ”uparjit punya nu bandhi punya”.
